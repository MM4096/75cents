Relatives

I am related to other fish such as trout, grayling and whitefish

My Adaptations

I am born in rivers, and moves to the sea later on in life. I am adapted to cope with both freshwater and seawater.
I usually spend a year or two, before I migrate back to my home stream.
My body shape allows me to escape predators (mainly seals and birds) faster, and also to hunt quicker. I also have a line on my body that can sense which way the current is going, so I know where to go to migrate.

Where I live

I live in the North Atlantic Ocean, and also in the Pasific.

What I need to survive

I need cold water to survive, but now to cold, as I could freeze. I control the number of shrimp in the ocean, as well as small fish.
